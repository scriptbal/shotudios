Terms of Service

Please read the following information before placing an order.

GENERAL

Please include SHOTUDIOS as the Live2D rigger. After your debut, we may request permission to showcase the finished model as part of our portfolio.

PAYMENTS

There is no full refund after the project has started. If the project cannot be completed by us, partially completed source files and a partial refund will be discussed.

REVISIONS

Every package includes revisions according to the selected plan. Bugs caused by us will always be fixed free of charge.

DEADLINE

Estimated production time is around 2–3 weeks depending on model complexity and current commission queue.

COMMUNICATION

Communication is available through Discord, Email, VGen, or any platform agreed upon by both parties.

Yes. As long as the PSD is properly layered and ready for Live2D workflow.

Yes. Every package is optimized for VTube Studio, PRPRLive, Warudo and similar software.

Absolutely. Additional expressions can be ordered anytime.
Do you accept commercial projects?
Yes. We work with individual creators, agencies, studios, and companies.
